How to measure your ring size

  • Use a thread or ribbon and wrap it around the base of your finger. The thread or ribbon should be thin enough to accurately follow the contour of your finger. Ensure the thread or ribbon lies snugly against the skin without constricting your finger.
  • Mark the point where the end of the thread or ribbon meets the rest of the thread or ribbon.
  • Orders are processed and shipped within 7 business days.
  • Shipping is via DHL and typically takes 2-3 business days from the shipping date.

tips

  1. Measure your finger at the end of the day.
  2. Do not measure if your hands are too cold or too warm.
  3. Keep in mind that the fingers on both hands can be slightly different sizes.
